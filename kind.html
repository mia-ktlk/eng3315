Be Kind

Not a body type Not a gender
Not a phase
Not a diet
Not for attention
Not a choice
Not a trend

Are everywhere
Are harmful
Are encouraged by society
Are isolating
Are masqueraded as healthy
Are fatal

Diet culture and fatphobia are so heavily intertwined with our culture. Society has warped the perception of health and beauty so horridly that people are killing themselves in the pursuit of the perfect picture of health. THERE IS NO WRONG WAY TO HAVE A BODY. There can be health at any size. “I will do 30 minutes of exercise to earn my dessert” “Wow, that’s a large plate of food. Are you really going to eat all of that?” “This brand of ice cream is guilt-free” “Friday’s are my cheat day” I have seen the horrors of an eating disorder and I have lost someone to it. The media we consume, the language we use, the subconscious thoughts we have are all drenched in ideology promoting eating disorders. It is everywhere… slowly gnawing on us convincing us to take up less space until eventually, we disappear. Be kind to yourself.
